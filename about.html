About Daniel Bower

Hello everyone!

My name is Daniel Bower (Dan) and I previously studied at the University of Manchester doing an undergraduate degree in Politics and Economics. However I graduated in 2006 so I think that makes me a mature student!

I did some "bedroom coding" when I was younger but haven't done any since then despite working quite close to software engineers during my career. I am excited about the opportunity to do some coding again.

I recently got back from Puglia and saw a fantastic exhibition of Gianni Berengo Gardin whose photos of Italy in the 50s and 60s are particularly wonderful.
